Frequently Asked Questions
How do I track my order?

You can track your order in multiple ways:

  • Via Account: Go to My Orders → Track Order for real-time updates
  • Via Email/SMS: Once shipped, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS
  • Order Status Flow: Pending → Processing → Shipped → Out for Delivery → Delivered
  • Mobile App: Download our app for push notifications

📦 Delivery Time: Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days. Express delivery takes 2-3 business days.

❓ Still can't find your order? Contact our support team with your order ID.

What is your return & refund policy?

We offer a 7-day easy return policy for:

  • ✓ Damaged or defective books (torn pages, printing errors)
  • ✓ Incorrect items shipped (wrong book or edition)
  • ✓ Wrong quantity delivered
  • ✓ Missing pages or binding defects

Return Process:

  1. Go to My Orders → Return Item
  2. Select reason and upload photos (if applicable)
  3. Our team will verify within 24 hours
  4. Schedule a free pickup or self-ship (reimbursement provided)

Refund Timeline:

  • 💳 Credit/Debit Card: 5-7 business days
  • 📱 UPI/Net Banking: 3-5 business days
  • 💵 COD: Store credit or bank transfer (7-10 days)
  • 🎁 Gift Card: Instant refund to gift card

⚠️ Note: Books must be unused and in original packaging for return.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery Options & Timeframes:

  • 🚚 Standard Delivery: 5-7 business days (Free on orders above ₹499)
  • ⚡ Express Delivery: 2-3 business days (₹99 extra - available in metro cities)
  • 🏃 Same-Day Delivery: Order before 12 PM for delivery by 8 PM (Available in: Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Chennai, Kolkata, Hyderabad, Pune)
  • 🌍 International Shipping: 10-15 business days (Available for select countries)

Tracking Updates: You'll receive updates at:

  • Order Confirmed (immediate)
  • Order Processed (within 24 hours)
  • Shipped with tracking number (1-2 days)
  • Out for Delivery (day of delivery)
  • Delivered (confirmation)

📞 Delayed delivery? Contact us if your order hasn't arrived within the promised timeframe.

Is there free shipping available?

Free Shipping Policy:

  • ✅ Free Standard Shipping on all orders above ₹499
  • 📦 Flat ₹40 Shipping Fee for orders below ₹499
  • 🚚 Express Shipping: ₹99 extra (2-3 days delivery)
  • 🎉 Festive Offers: Free shipping on all orders during sale periods
  • ⭐ Prime Members: Unlimited free express shipping (Subscribe for ₹499/year)

How to check if you qualify:

  • Add items to cart and proceed to checkout
  • Shipping charges will be displayed before payment
  • Apply coupon codes for additional shipping discounts

🏆 Pro tip: Add an extra book to cross ₹499 and save on shipping!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a wide range of payment methods:

💳 Cards:

  • Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, RuPay)
  • Debit Cards (All major banks)
  • Prepaid Cards & Gift Cards

📱 Digital Wallets & UPI:

  • Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, Amazon Pay
  • BHIM UPI, WhatsApp Pay
  • Any UPI app (QR code payment)

🏦 Net Banking:

  • SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Yes Bank, Kotak, and 50+ banks

💵 Cash on Delivery (COD):

  • Available in 19,000+ pin codes
  • Maximum order value for COD: ₹5,000
  • Convenience fee: ₹30 per COD order

🔄 EMI Options:

  • No-cost EMI on orders above ₹3,000
  • 3, 6, 9, 12 months tenure available
  • Available on Credit Cards from leading banks

🔒 Secure Payments: All transactions are encrypted and PCI compliant.

Can I cancel my order?

✅ Free cancellation is available with these timelines:

Before Shipment (within 1 hour of order):

  • Full refund automatically processed
  • Cancel from My Orders → Cancel Order
  • Money refunded within 3-5 business days

After Shipment (before delivery):

  • Cancel within 1 hour of shipping confirmation
  • Return accepted when delivery is attempted
  • No need to accept delivery

After Delivery:

  • Use our 7-day return policy
  • Full refund after quality check
  • Free pickup arranged from your address

Special Cases:

  • 📚 Pre-ordered books: Cancel anytime before release for full refund
  • 🎯 Sale items: Returnable unless marked "Final Sale"
  • 📖 Digital books: Non-refundable after download

How to Cancel: Go to My Orders → Select Order → Cancel Order or contact support.

How do I write a review for a book?

Eligibility: You can write a review only after:

  • ✓ Purchasing and receiving the book
  • ✓ Order status is "Delivered"
  • ✓ Within 30 days of delivery

Steps to write a review:

  1. Go to My Orders section
  2. Find the delivered order (look for "Write a Review" button)
  3. Rate the book from 1-5 stars (click on stars)
  4. Add a title (e.g., "Amazing read!", "Could be better")
  5. Write your detailed review (minimum 10 words)
  6. Optionally, upload photos of the book
  7. Click Submit Review

Review Guidelines:

  • ✍️ Be honest and constructive
  • 📸 Add photos to help other buyers
  • 🚫 No offensive language or personal attacks
  • 📚 Focus on content quality, binding, paper quality
  • ✅ Verified purchase badge appears on your review

Rewards for reviewing: Earn 10 loyalty points for each review (max 5 per month). 100 points = ₹10 off!

My book arrived damaged. What should I do?

Don't worry! We've got you covered. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Document the damage

  • 📸 Take clear, well-lit photos of the damaged book
  • 📸 Show the packaging if it's also damaged
  • 📸 Take close-ups of specific defects (tears, stains, missing pages)

Step 2: Initiate return/replacement

  1. Go to My Orders
  2. Click Return/Replace on the affected order
  3. Select reason: "Damaged Product"
  4. Upload the photos you took
  5. Add brief description of the damage
  6. Choose "Replacement" (preferred) or "Refund"

Step 3: What happens next?

  • ✅ Our team verifies within 24 hours
  • ✅ Replacement: New book shipped within 2 days (free)
  • ✅ Refund: Processed after returns team checks the book
  • ✅ Free pickup arranged from your address

Processing Time:

  • Replacement: 5-7 days for new book delivery
  • Refund: 5-10 business days to original payment method

📞 Urgent? Call our support for immediate assistance: 1800-XXX-XXXX

I'm having trouble with my account. What do I do?

Common account issues and solutions:

🔑 Forgot Password:

  1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
  2. Enter registered email/phone number
  3. Check email/SMS for OTP/reset link
  4. Create new password (minimum 8 characters with 1 number)

📧 Email Change:

  • Go to Account Settings → Email Preferences
  • Verify current password
  • Enter new email and verify via OTP

📱 Mobile Number Change:

  • Account Settings → Mobile Number
  • Request OTP on current number
  • Enter new number and verify OTP

🔒 Account Locked:

  • Too many failed login attempts → Wait 30 minutes
  • Contact support to unlock immediately
  • Use "Forgot Password" to reset and regain access

👤 Profile Update:

  • Change name, profile picture, preferences
  • Manage address book (add/edit/delete addresses)
  • Set default shipping/billing address
  • Update communication preferences (email/SMS notifications)

🗑️ Account Deletion:

  • Contact support to request account deletion
  • Verification required (ID proof)
  • Processing time: 30 days for complete deletion

Still stuck? Live chat with our support team for instant help!

How do I apply promo codes or coupons?

How to apply coupons:

  1. Add items to your cart
  2. Go to Cart or Checkout page
  3. Look for "Apply Coupon Code" or "Gift Card" section
  4. Enter your promo code (case insensitive)
  5. Click "Apply" - discount will be reflected immediately

Types of discounts available:

  • 🎯 Site-wide sales (automatically applied)
  • 📚 Category-specific (e.g., 20% off Fiction)
  • 💰 Minimum purchase (e.g., ₹100 off on ₹999+)
  • 🆕 First order (NEW10 for 10% off on first purchase)
  • ⭐ Loyalty rewards (earn points, redeem for discounts)
  • 🎉 Festive specials (limited time codes)

Coupon Rules:

  • One coupon per order (cannot combine multiple)
  • Valid on minimum purchase amount (if specified)
  • Check expiry date - most codes valid for 7-30 days
  • Not applicable on already discounted items (some exceptions)

Where to find codes?

  • 📧 Email newsletters (subscribe on homepage)
  • 📱 Push notifications from our app
  • 🎯 Social media (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter)
  • 🎁 Welcome emails for new users
  • ⭐ Loyalty program rewards page

Troubleshooting: Code not working? Check minimum order value, expiry date, and applicable categories.
